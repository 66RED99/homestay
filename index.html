Intro

i - One's Home Stay is located in Kochi. The popular attractions of the Fort Kochi Beach is 500 metres and Mattancherry Palace and Jewish Synagogue are within 2 km.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. The accommodation will provide you a desk and a seating area. Private bathrooms also come with a shower.

At i - One's Home Stay you will find a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage. Services like laundry and dry cleaning are provided. The Chinese Fishing Net is 500 metres away. The Cochin Bus Station and the Ernakulam Railway Station are within 12 km from the property. The Cochin International Airport is 40 km. The in-house dining area serves Regional and local specialities. Room service is available.
